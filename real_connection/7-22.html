Finding God

[…] finding God […] is not the result of a search at all, but of a moral housecleaning (SA 102).


How does my Higher Power come into my life? Imagine a glass filled to the top with water. There is no air in the glass because the water takes up all the space. If I want to get air into the glass, I have to pour out some of the water. Air naturally fills the void left by the water.


So it is with my relationship with God. God is the air, and my self-will is the water. As I become willing to empty myself of destructive sexual habits and surrender self-centered attitudes, God fills me with the courage to act.


Today I know why I could not stop acting out for so many years. I was filled with lust. I did not want to change, but my life became so unmanageable that I turned to SA in desperation. In the program, I learned to surrender my lustful will to God in Steps One, Two, and Three. I found his power enabled me to surrender my harmful behavior, and to work the remaining Steps that helped me make a moral housecleaning. Today my life is immeasurably better because I find God filling my glass with serenity, humility, joy, and opportunities to be of service to others.


Higher Power, help me to keep my ego in check and therefore leave open space for You.
